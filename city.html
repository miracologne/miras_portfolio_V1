Cologne City

Cologne is an independent city in the state of North Rhine-Westphalia with about 1.1 million inhabitants.

The city is considered one of the most important tourist destinations in Europe mainly because of the important Cologne Cathedral and its Romanesque churches.

The Cologne Carnival is an important part of Cologne's culture and always begins on November 11 at 11:11 am.

Cologne is a colorful and very tolerant city and definitely worth a visit.
